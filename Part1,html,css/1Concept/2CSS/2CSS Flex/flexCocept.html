Flex-Parent Properties:-

  1. flex-direction:- row, rowreverse,column and column-reverse, (gives direction rowWise or columnWise)
  2. justify-content:-flex-start,flex-end,center,space-between,space-around,space-evenly.(Everything Horizontally)
  3. align-items:-flex-start | flex-end | center | baseline | stretch;(Everything Vertically)
  4. flex-wrap:- wrap,nowrap,wrap-revese,(it wraps the items)

Flex-Child Properties:-

1.align-self:- overrides alignment set on parent
2.flex-grow:- (Defaults to 0)
3.flex-shrink:- (Defaults to 1)
4.order
5.flex-grow
6.flex-shrink
7.flex-basis
