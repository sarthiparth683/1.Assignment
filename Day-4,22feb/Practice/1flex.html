Flex-Parent Properties:-
1.flex-direction:- (Direction) to all child to (Row-Wise and Column Wise), row, rowreverse,column and column-reverse
2.flex-wrap:- it wraps the items inside Parent div, wrap,nowrap,wrap-revese
3.justify-content:-(horizontally) all child to (main axis, horizontally):- flex-start,flex-end,center,space-between,space-around,space-evenly.
4.align-items:-(Vertically) to all child to (cross axis,vertically): flex-start | flex-end | center | baseline | stretch;
5.align-content:- flex-start | flex-end | center | stretch | space-between | space-around | space-evenly; [wrapping scenario]
6.flex-flow:-mix of wrap and direction, (shorthand for flex-direction and flex-wrap)

Flex-Child Properties:-
1.align-self:- overrides alignment set on parent
2.flex-grow:- (Defaults to 0)
3.flex-shrink:- (Defaults to 1)
4.order
5.flex-grow
6.flex-shrink
